We’re Better. Here’s Why…

Patowave exists to build a digital ecosystem for African MSMEs and its community, by transforming methods of works and reinventing products that create new values, connect them with things, insights and bring them new experiences to make their life better and easier. Patowave believes in the future of work, as a positive response to the fundamental needs of African MSMEs, by presenting simplicity in the methods of work that promotes efficiency. We harness the power of technology to empower African MSMEs to become financially aware and improve their decision making for their long-term business success.

Our Values

We are not predicting the future; we are building it. Our values hold us together on what we stand for as a team, who we are and what we believe in. We keep innovating our way forward to always deliver the best products to our customers. We are helping each other do better by promoting inclusive innovations inside and outside of our company. We admit and correct ourselves when we are wrong. We give our customers opportunities to collaborate closely with our team and help the team create the best solutions that meet their expectations and make them feel proud with positive perceptions and better feelings towards our company and products. These values shape our culture and remind us why we do what we do daily.

Our Core Values

We have a product driven mentality; we use our products to set up the path of where the company is headed. Our mission to digitize African MSMEs, is what gives us the purpose to invent great products and solutions. Our values guide us through fundamental beliefs and help us function together as a team toward the company goals. At Patowave we believe that productivity can be achieved through simplicity, and anybody can be productive with better tools.
